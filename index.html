Theis Construction Panels (SIP) — The Technology

Theis Construction Panels are Structural Insulated Panels (SIP) that combine multiple construction layers into a single, all-in-one building system. They replace traditional wall framing, roof framing, weather barriers, insulation, and exterior cladding — all in one panel.

⚡

60% Faster Construction

Panels can be pre-manufactured or fabricated directly on-site, drastically reducing labor, installation time, and cost vs. traditional stick-frame construction.

🛡️

Category 5 Hurricane Rated

Fire and impact-resistant, seamless waterproof barrier, UV-stable, high wind protection, corrosion-resistant. Critical for coastal NE properties.

🌡️

Tekton Foam Insulation

Customizable R-values to suit any climate. Airtight insulation reduces heating/cooling costs, enhancing energy efficiency and structural integrity.

🎨

Any Exterior Finish

Each panel achieves any desired exterior finish — wood, stone, stucco, brick, metal. Design flexibility tailored to local coastal aesthetics.

🔧

Pre-Fitted Systems

Flexible options for pre-installed HVAC, plumbing, and electrical built directly into panels. Reduces on-site trades and coordination time.

🌿

Zero VOCs — Eco-Friendly

No volatile organic compounds, minimal waste, reduced environmental impact. Long-lasting performance with low maintenance — ideal for healthcare environments.
