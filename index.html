The Founder's Vision
Bridging Healthcare & Technology
R.E.A.L. Holdings exists at the intersection of healthcare access, AI innovation, and community development.
We believe healthcare technology should serve the underserved first. Every system we build, every partnership we forge, starts with the communities that need it most.
Jake Clopton, Founder & CEO

The Problem

Fragmented healthcare IT systems leave FQHCs and rural providers behind. EHR vendors charge premium prices for outdated solutions while underserved communities lack modern telehealth and AI-driven care.

Our Solution

A vertically integrated platform combining Clinix AI (healthcare IT), Clopton Capital (real estate), and grant-funded community infrastructure to deliver affordable, AI-powered healthcare technology at scale.
