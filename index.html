Competitive Moats
01

FQHC-Specific AI

Purpose-built clinical AI for Federally Qualified Health Centers — no competitor targets this niche with dedicated technology. 1,400+ center addressable market with zero viable alternatives.

02

18-Pillar Revenue Diversification

Unlike single-product startups, R.E.A.L. Holdings deploys 18 interlocking revenue streams. Loss of any single stream has <15% impact on total revenue.

03

$5.3B+ Non-Dilutive Capital Access

Proprietary grant pipeline with 445 identified programs. Grant revenue reduces equity dilution and accelerates growth without additional investor capital.

04

Regulatory & Compliance Expertise

Deep domain knowledge in HRSA requirements, 340B compliance, HIPAA, and healthcare IT regulations creates a significant barrier to entry for generalist competitors.

05

Integrated Vertical Stack

End-to-end solution from EHR to billing to pharmacy to analytics. Switching costs increase with each module adopted, creating strong customer lock-in.
